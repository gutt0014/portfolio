About Me

My name is Kaitlynn Guttridge, I am a student at Algonquin College currently taking Interactive Media Design, graduating in 2025. I got into designing when I was in high school but first tried to take my creative mind into the baking world. I am a trained pastry chef and still love the art of baking but ultimately my heart belongs in the digital design world.

I have a passion for creating clean, fun and unique designs which can range from logos, social media posts, business cards, brochures, and more.
